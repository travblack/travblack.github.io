The retail price of sports equipment is too high...

Many families love watching and supporting their kids participate in sports at every level. As one progresses in his sport, higher quality equipment is almost always a necessity in order to excel. The problem for many athletic families today comes from the expensive price tag that comes with these pieces of equipment. 

Our Promise

We here at Blackwell INC. know the struggle of parents and athletes to find the top of the line gear for affordable prices, which is why we exist. Blackwell INC. is committed to all those who compete in the beautiful world of sports. We promise to provide the cheapest, yet quality sports gear in the Mid-Michigan area to provide the path for every developing athlete to pursue his or her dream. 

What do we carry?

Blackwell INC. in Grand Blanc carries almost every convential sport that is available in the area from all four Michigan seasons. These include baseball, basketball, soccer, football, golf, bowling, snowboarding/skiing, and more.
